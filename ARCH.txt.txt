B-machine architecture
----------------------

The architecture of B-machine is simple:
* A-stack with 7 elements 64-bit
* ?? E-registers: 7 register for the expanded real (80-bit)
* BF register: for the bit operations

The machine can operate with 16, 32 and 64 bits data formats.
It uses an integer ariphmetics, real ariphmetics
and boolean logic.

BF[0] is used as RLO for the boolena operations.

+----------+------------+-----------------------------------+

+----------+------------+-----------------------------------+
